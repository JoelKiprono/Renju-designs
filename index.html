Milly Renju

Designer. Stylist. Model.

Renju is a passionate fashion designer with great skills in cloth design and modelling. With a Diploma in Fashion Design from RVIST and a Degree in Product design, she has handled fashion projects in Kenya, London and Italy. She is the current holder of Miss Kenya 2025 title. Renju is blessed with both beauty and brains. She is much loved.
